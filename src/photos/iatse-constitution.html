IATSE Projectionists Constitution & Bylaws

In 2021, my partner and I were in a thrift store in Wichita, KS and found this copy of Local 164 of the Motion Picture Projectionists Union of Milwaukee, Wisconsin. This copy is the revised 1926 copy.

After taking photos, I mailed it to the IATSE archivist so the union can research and preserve this small piece of history.

The photos aren't that great, maybe some day I'll be able to edit them.
